Our Advisor Panel

The Barishal University Physics Club is proud to have a diverse and esteemed Advisor Panel comprising faculty members from various departments. Their collective expertise, guidance, and support play a crucial role in shaping our club’s activities and initiatives. These advisors bring a wealth of knowledge and experience from different fields, fostering interdisciplinary collaboration and enriching our understanding of physics. Their dedication helps us create a vibrant, inclusive, and intellectually stimulating environment for all our members. Together, we strive to explore the frontiers of science and inspire the next generation of physicists.
